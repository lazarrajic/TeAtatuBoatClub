# Committee revision meeting — decisions needed

One page. Each item is a decision the committee can make in the room. ✦ = my recommendation.

## Booking system

1. **Large-vessel (10m+) price** — a big boat takes 2 bays for the day. Does it pay **1× or 2× the member day rate?** (The system is built; it just needs the number.)
2. **Bay names** — confirm the four names. They must be numbered **in physical order**, and both pairs (1+2 and 3+4) must each physically fit a 10m+ boat.
3. **Second large-vessel pair** — confirm 10m+ boats may use **bays 3+4 as well as 1+2** (means two big boats can book the same day). Build is ready to go once confirmed.
4. **Booking emails** — confirmations to the member + notifications to the office are built but dormant. To switch on we need **DNS access to the club's domain** (Dan) — ~10 min job.
5. **Payment for bookings** — ✦ keep office invoicing (current behaviour). Online payment is possible but adds cost/complexity for little gain at club volume.
